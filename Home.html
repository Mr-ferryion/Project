About Us

Who we are

Risaka Group is a dynamic and emerging enterprise, proudly owned and managed by Black entrepreneurs who bring extensive professional experience in the telecommunications sector in South Africa. As a Level 1 BEE enterprise with 60% Black female ownership, we provide our clients with significant transformation benefits on their BEE Scorecards. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our adherence to the highest standards of health, safety, environmental, and quality practices in all our project implementations.

What we do

We possess the in-house capabilities to deliver comprehensive turnkey solutions, including site acquisition and permitting, regularization, passive infrastructure construction, radio network installation and commissioning, power solution provision, and effective community engagement. Our power solutions encompass battery installations, municipal and Eskom applications, account reconciliations, and statement inquiries with the relevant authorities.

Our Services

Site Acquisition, Permitting & Regularisation

We leverage our established influence and relationships with municipalities, traditional councils, property developers, and other stakeholders to optimize processes and deliver expedited outcomes.

Property Leasing

We boast a dedicated team of experienced negotiators specializing in lease renewals, cessions, and lease exits. With Our profound expertise in traditional processes and protocols is bolstered by direct access and longstanding relationships with local Chiefs and Traditional Councils.

Construction, Maintenance & Project Maintence

Our company specializes in delivering efficient, safe, and cost-effective civil and tower construction services. We cater specifically to large wireless carriers and industry-leading tower owners, offering expertise in raw land tower builds and co-locations. Our dedicated EF&I professionals oversee the entire construction process, from initial site planning and RF engineering through to comprehensive system testing
